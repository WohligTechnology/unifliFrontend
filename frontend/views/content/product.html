  • 448 hardware channels for simultaneous tracking of all visible satellite signals:
    • GPS: L1, L2, L5• GLONASS: L1, L2, L3• Galileo: E1, E5a, E5b, AltBoc 1• BeiDou: B1, B21• SBAS: EGNOS, WAAS, GAGAN, MSAS, SDCM L1, L5• IRNSS: L51• QZSS: L1, L2, L5
